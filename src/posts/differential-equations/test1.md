$$y_1^{\prime}y_2-y_1y_2^\prime=Ce^{-\int p(x)dx}$$ 
$$ f(x)=\left\{
\begin{aligned}
y_1^{\prime}y_2-y_1y_2^\prime \neq0 \Rightarrow & y_1,y_2 \;\text{线性无关}
\\
y_1^{\prime}y_2-y_1y_2^\prime =0 \Rightarrow & y_1,y_2 \;\text{线性相关}
\end{aligned}
