# 微分方程结论

#### 1.

$y^{''}+p(x)y{'}+q(x)y=0$ 的任二解$y_1(x),y_2(x)$ 必满足：
$$y_1^{\prime}y_2-y_1y_2^\prime=Ce^{-\int p(x)dx}$$ 
$$ f(x)=\left\{
\begin{aligned}
y_1^{\prime}y_2-y_1y_2^\prime \neq0 \Rightarrow & y_1,y_2 线性无关
\\
y_1^{\prime}y_2-y_1y_2^\prime =0 \Rightarrow & y_1,y_2 线性相关
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$
